Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omalette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables or meat.

    Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Prepoaration: 5 minutes
  • cooking: 5 minutes

    Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetable ,cooked meat,herbs

    Instruction

  1. Beat the egg : In a bowel, beat the eggs
    with a pinch of salt and pepper untill they are well mixed.
    You can add a table spoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubling pour in the eggs.
    Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add filling (optional): When the eggs begins to set at the edge
    but are still slighly runny in the middle sprinklebr your choosen filling over one half of the omelette
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continue to cook,
    carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filings. Let it cook for another minute,
    then slide it unto a plate
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with additional salt and pepper if needed.
